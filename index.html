Quick Tips for Americans

Every letter is pronounced

Unlike English, Turkish has no silent letters. Every single letter is spoken — always consistently.

Vowels are pure

Turkish vowels don't glide. "A" is always like in "spa", never like in "cake". Short and clean.

Stress is light

Turkish stress is gentle — usually on the last syllable. Don't hammer it like English.

No TH, W, or X

These English sounds don't exist in Turkish. The alphabet fills those gaps with unique letters like Ş and Ğ.
